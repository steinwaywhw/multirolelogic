Multirole Logic

Author: Hongwei Xi (@githwxi) and Hanwen Wu (@steinwaywhw)

© 2016 Hongwei Xi and Hanwen Wu, All Rights Reserved.

Abstract

We identify multirole logic as a new form of logic and formalize both dual classical multirole logic (DCML) and dual classical linear multirole logic (DCLML) as natural generalizations of classical logic (CL) and classical linear logic (CLL), respectively. Among various meta-properties established for DCML and DCLML, we obtain one stating that every cut between three (or more) sequents (as a generalization of a cut between two sequents) can be elmininated, thus extending the celebrated result of cut-elimination by Gentzen.
